Montra is a specialized Payment Facilitator (PayFac) and billing platform designed specifically for AI-first companies. We handle the complexities of modern AI business models, including high-frequency usage events and hybrid pricing structures.

## Key Features

- **Real-time Metering**: Report usage events at scale.
- **Hybrid Billing**: Mix seat-based and usage-based models seamlessly.
- **Developer SDKs**: Unified TypeScript SDK for Node.js, React, Next.js, and Express.
- **Secure Checkout**: Hosted and embedded checkout sessions.
